About Me

My name is Bethan James, I am 25 years old from South West Wales and currently living in Manchester. I have recently started a bootcamp at the University with the hope to start a career in the tech industry as a web developer.

How I got into web developing:

In 2015 I moved to Eastern Canada and work at a ski resort in the ticket office. The resort were implementing a resort wide software to use in the hotel, ticket office and different food outlets. I was added to the team working on inputting all the data and going through the training to use the different aspects of the software.

As part of the package it aksi included a shell website that customer could book hotel stays, season passes and tickets, gift cards and book lessons. It was a very basic website that didnt come with a lot of style or customization. The company wanted to make changes to get the website to work they wanted, this is when we started looking at the code. I didnt understand what I was looking at but after watching the web developer I realised I had an interest in the area.Over the last 2 years I have been teaching myself using online sources until I started the bootcamp.
